About

I help teams turn fuzzy ideas into products that ship.

Over the last six years I’ve partnered with founders, PMs and engineers across fintech, health and developer tools — translating ambiguity into thoughtful, usable interfaces.

I care about craft: the curve of a button, the rhythm of a screen, the small moments that make a product feel inevitable. When I’m not designing, you’ll find me sketching, learning ceramics, or hunting for the city’s best filter coffee.
